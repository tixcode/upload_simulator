What is Upload Simulator?

Upload Simulator is a incremental game about uploading files and upgrading your setup made via Godot Game Engine. Upload large files, gain reputation, research amazing technologies and explore sci-fi themed components while watching progress bars fill faster with each upgrade! You can reboot and research technologies to help your uploading journey. Get random events like NFTs popping, internet disconnections, trolls, or upload requests.

Get futuristic network components or 100TB SD Cards. You can choose to click or coast, in this satisfying incremental game!
