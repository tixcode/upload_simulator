What is Overclocking?

Overclocking is mechanic that allows you to "overclock" Components and multiple their efficiency by 2. To overclock a component you need certain amount of Overclocks.

Where I can overclock components?

In order to overclock Components, you must: unlock "Overclocker" perk and upgrade Component that you wanna overclock to the max level.
