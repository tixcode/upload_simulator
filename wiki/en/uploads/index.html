What are Uploads?

Uploads are one from the main feature of the game. I even can say that they are the most important part of Upload Simulator, because you can, well upload them and

get Credits after completing the upload. Not only Credits tho, Reputation too.

Each Upload has it own size. If your upload speed is too big, you need to complete upload many times to complete it, aka "Peers".
